Play to Glory - a Layer 2 for blockchain games

Most blockchain games chase money. We chase meaning. Tokamak Games is building Play to Glory: a Layer 2 designed to reinstate consequence inside the Magic Circle, and to make victory, defeat, and contribution permanent.
