01 / Abstract

Intrinsic play before economic extraction.

Whereas most blockchain games prioritize monetary rewards under Play-to-Earn, P2G emphasizes intrinsic enjoyment and immersive gameplay by reinstating meaningful risk within the Magic Circle.

Layer 2 is employed as enabling infrastructure to make outcomes durable and public on-chain, and to support sustainable, community-governed growth.

02 / Magic Circle

The game needs a boundary before it needs a market.

First articulated by Johan Huizinga, the Magic Circle is the boundary between reality and play. Inside it, ordinary rules are suspended; the game's rules alone govern behavior and meaning.

Within this protected space, players develop skill, experience achievement, and cultivate self-respect. Cooperation and competition produce belonging and shared purpose that outlast the match itself.

05 / Sports

Revenue belongs around the game, not inside its rules.

In sports, revenue is not extracted from the core game. The core remains skill-based and inviolable. Meta-games emerge around it: broadcasting, tournaments, betting, sponsorships, merchandise.

P2G applies the same architecture to blockchain games. Prediction markets, fan memberships, guild economies, and tournaments surround the core without corrupting it.

06 / Risk

The leaderboard should remember again.

The arcade era was defined by consequence: a limited number of lives and a leaderboard that remembered. Home consoles diluted the stakes; defeat lost its weight, and victory lost its meaning.

P2G restores stakes through persistence. Victories, defeats, and contributions are recorded on-chain: public, composable, permanent.
