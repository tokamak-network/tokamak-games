ABSTRACT

Play itself is the source of value.

Whereas most blockchain games prioritize monetary rewards under Play-to-Earn, P2G emphasizes intrinsic enjoyment and immersive gameplay by reinstating meaningful risk within the Magic Circle.

Layer 2 makes outcomes durable and public on-chain, and supports sustainable, community-governed growth.

02
CIRCLE

The Magic Circle is where the game still rules.

First articulated by Johan Huizinga, the Magic Circle is the boundary between reality and play. Inside it, the game's rules govern behavior and meaning.

P2G keeps play inside while durable outcomes move outward into public infrastructure.

05
SPORTS

Sports show the right architecture.

In sports, revenue is not extracted from the core game. The core remains skill-based and inviolable. Broadcasting, tournaments, betting, sponsorships, and merchandise emerge around it.

P2G applies the same architecture to blockchain games through prediction markets, fan memberships, guild economies, and tournaments.

Bring back the remembered run.

The arcade era was defined by consequence: limited lives and a leaderboard that remembered. P2G restores stakes through persistence.

Victories, defeats, and contributions are recorded on-chain: public, composable, permanent.
