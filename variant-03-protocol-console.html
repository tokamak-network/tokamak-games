01 / Abstract

Play itself is the source of value.

Whereas most blockchain games prioritize monetary rewards under Play-to-Earn, P2G emphasizes intrinsic enjoyment and immersive gameplay by reinstating meaningful risk within the Magic Circle.

Layer 2 is employed as enabling infrastructure to make outcomes durable and public on-chain, and to support sustainable, community-governed growth.

02 / Boundary

The Magic Circle is treated as a boundary layer.

First articulated by Johan Huizinga, the Magic Circle is the boundary between reality and play. Inside it, ordinary rules are suspended; the game's rules govern behavior and meaning.

Play stays inside. Durable outcomes move outward into public infrastructure.

05 / Sports

Meta-games surround the core instead of invading it.

In sports, revenue is not extracted from the core game. Broadcasting, tournaments, betting, sponsorships, and merchandise emerge around an inviolable skill-based core.

P2G applies the same architecture to blockchain games: prediction markets, fan memberships, guild economies, and tournaments compose around the record.

06 / Risk

Persistence restores consequence.

The arcade era was defined by consequence: limited lives and a leaderboard that remembered. P2G restores stakes through persistence.

Victories, defeats, and contributions are recorded on-chain: public, composable, permanent. The goal is to make play matter.
